Cards & Panels

Base

Plain Card

Default card with 1px border on #E3E8F2, white background, 4px radius, 16px padding. The foundational surface for content grouping.

Elevated

Elevated Card

Card with blue-gray shadow (0 2px 4px rgba(43,57,79,0.12)) and transparent border. Used for interactive or prominent surfaces.

Subdued

Subdued Card

Background matches page (#F6F9FC) for recessed, secondary content. Maintains the same border and radius.

Semantic

Primary Panel

Semantic background (#F1F6FF) with matching border (#BFDBFF). Used for contextual callouts and highlighted content areas.
